About Me

I graduated with a bachelors degree in finance, and have worked for various investment companies as an analyst. Some of my hobbies include playing guitar and piano, shooting billiards, and exercising. My favorite sport is boxing. I try watch every boxing event I can throughout the year. Come football season, I'm a diehard Miami Dolphins and Miami Hurricanes fan.
